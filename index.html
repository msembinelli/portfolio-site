About

Matthew Sembinelli is a self­proclaimed tinkerer and technology buff. His interest in computers started at 13 years of age and it quickly turned into a career path. Matthew has strong interests in embedded systems, digital logic, and programming. He is currently employed in the low power wireless industry, focusing on MCUs and radio stack development. Matt is proficient in several programming languages, including C/C++, Python, Java, HTML/CSS, and Javascript. Matthew can sometimes be found performing PC overclocking experiments with liquid nitrogen and dry ice.
